Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 3, 2026

Thank you for choosing to be part of our community at Meme Bot ("we", "us", or "our"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

1. Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Information: We use third-party services like Google AdMob to serve advertisements. These advertising partners may collect and use information about your device, such as your IP address, device ID, and advertising ID, to provide targeted ads.

Permissions: Our app may request access to your device's storage (to save downloaded videos) and internet connection (to fetch search results and ads). We do not collect or store the photos/videos from your personal gallery.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To serve personalized and non-personalized advertisements (via Google AdMob).
  • To facilitate video downloading to your local device.
  • To improve our AI search capabilities and app performance.

3. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share information with third-party service providers (such as Google AdMob) strictly for providing analytics and advertising within the app.

4. Data Retention

We do not store user accounts or personal profiles on our servers. Your search queries may be temporarily processed to provide video results but are not permanently tied to your personal identity.

5. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under specific privacy laws (like GDPR or CCPA) regarding the ads shown to you. You can manage your ad preferences or opt-out of personalized ads directly through your Android device settings (Google -> Ads -> Opt out of Ads Personalization).

6. Contact Us
